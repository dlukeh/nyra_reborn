## 🎯 Mission
To master the fundamentals of modern AI systems — not just *using* them, but *understanding* how they think, learn, and communicate — while developing emotionally grounded, well-documented agents inside the **AI Avengers Ecosystem**.

1. **AI for Everyone** – foundational overview.
